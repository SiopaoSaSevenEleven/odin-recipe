Perfect Hard-Boiled Eggs

Description

Achieving the perfect hard-boiled egg is a culinary art that requires precision and timing. The goal is to have a firm white and a perfectly cooked yolk, with no hint of green or rubberiness.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Â¼ cup distilled white vinegar
  • 6 cups water
  • 8 large eggs

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine salt, vinegar, and water in a large pot, and bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, being careful not to crack them. Reduce the heat to a gentle boil, and cook for 14 minutes.
  4. Enjoy!
